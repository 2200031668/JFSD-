Frequently Asked Questions

What is CareerLink?

CareerLink is an online platform that connects individuals with career resources, services, and opportunities to enhance their professional growth.

How can I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and filling out the required information.

What services does CareerLink offer?

CareerLink offers various services, including job recommendations, resume reviews, career counseling, and webinars on professional development.

How do I reset my password?

If you forget your password, click on the 'Forgot Password?' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

Can I access CareerLink on my mobile device?

Yes, CareerLink is mobile-friendly and can be accessed on any device with internet connectivity.

Who can I contact for further support?

If you have any further questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our customer support team via the Contact Us page.
